Fight For The City

The Battle of Britain was lost. The Luftwaffe streamed across the channel and have begun the assault of London. The city is currently divided into Allied and Axis, separated by fierce fighting attempting to advance the no-mans-land between the two forces.

Stay in our themed resort as you eat and sleep in a recreation of a WWII operating base, while entering next-generation Virtual Reality to join the battle for the heart of England.
